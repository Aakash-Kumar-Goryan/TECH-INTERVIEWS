| **Self-motivated** | Internal drive to learn |
| **Goal-oriented** | Clear career/personal objectives |
| **Time-constrained** | Balance work, family, study |

## 9.2 Self-Directed Learning (SDL)

### Definition

**SDL** = Process where learners take initiative in diagnosing needs, formulating goals, identifying resources, implementing strategies, and evaluating outcomes.

### Knowles' Andragogy Principles

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Self-concept** | Adults need autonomy |
| **Experience** | Prior learning as resource |
| **Readiness** | Learn for real-life needs |
| **Orientation** | Problem-centered learning |
| **Motivation** | Internal motivation stronger |

### Stages of Self-Directed Learning (Grow's Model)

| Stage | Learner Type | Teacher Role |
|-------|--------------|--------------|
| 1 | Dependent | Authority/Coach |
| 2 | Interested | Motivator/Guide |
| 3 | Involved | Facilitator |
| 4 | Self-Directed | Consultant/Delegator |

# Unit 10: Counselling & Tutoring in ODE

## 10.1 Role of Counselling in ODL

### Types of Counselling

| Type | Focus |
|------|-------|
| **Academic** | Subject matter, study skills |
| **Administrative** | Admission, registration, exams |
| **Personal** | Motivation, stress, time management |
| **Career** | Job guidance, skill development |

### Functions of Academic Counsellor

- Explain difficult concepts
- Guide study strategies
- Monitor progress
- Provide feedback on assignments
- Motivate learners

---

## 10.2 Tutoring in Distance Education

### Difference: Tutoring vs Teaching

| Tutoring | Teaching |
|----------|----------|
| Facilitating | Transmitting |
| Clarifying SLMs | Creating content |
| Supporting | Directing |
| Individual focus | Group focus |
| Reactive | Proactive |

### Types of Tutoring Sessions

- **Face-to-face** at study centers
- **Online tutoring** via video/chat
- **Telephone tutoring**
- **Email support**

---

## 10.3 Role of Assignments

### Purpose of Assignments

1. **Continuous assessment** of learning
2. **Feedback** to learners
3. **Pacing** study activities
4. **Interaction** with tutor
5. **Preparation** for term-end exam

### Assignment Marking Principles

- Timely feedback (within 15 days)
- Constructive comments
- Clear evaluation criteria
- Encouraging tone
