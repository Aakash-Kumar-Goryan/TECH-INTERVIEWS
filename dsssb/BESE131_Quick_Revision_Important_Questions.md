## ODL vs Conventional Education

| Aspect | ODL | Conventional |
|--------|-----|--------------|
| Location | Anywhere | Fixed campus |
| Time | Flexible | Fixed schedule |
| Pace | Self-paced | Teacher-paced |
| Entry | Open/Flexible | Strict criteria |
| Materials | SLMs, multimedia | Lectures, notes |
| Interaction | Mediated | Direct |
| Cost per student | Lower (at scale) | Higher |

## Single-Mode vs Dual-Mode

| Aspect | Single-Mode | Dual-Mode |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| Programs | Only ODL | Both ODL & Regular |
| Resources | Dedicated | Shared |
| Examples | IGNOU, OU UK | Delhi University DDE |
| Focus | Distance learners only | Both types |
